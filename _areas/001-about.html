A little bit about our journey!

Hi, I'm Yarny (Ru) and along with my son, the Ant, I run forest school in Fareham.
So how did we get here?
I qualified as a secondary school teacher many moons ago, when my 3 boys were tiny and while I loved being with teenagers, I missed my own family. I moved to teaching adults and gained a little move work-life balance, until the COVID lockdowns hit and balancing anything became impossible. I had 3 children, at 3 different schools, with 3 completely different sets of needs, including complex learning and social support needs, as well as trying to work and keep the house vaguely together.
When children started to head back into the classroom, we realised that now was the perfect time to change things up. No more coasting along for our mini-mathematician, no more social-struggles for the Ant. Lots of time outdoors, lots more learning and following our interests, lots of success.
We started attending a forest school for teens with the amazing Debbie Dragonfly. It was such a brilliant experience and it fitted so well with our approach to education. It was just as much a transformational experience for the adults as for the children.
Debbie encourage me to do my Forest School Leader qualification, and I started in May 2024. I'm just finishing up my last couple of assignments now (April 2025).
So here I am leading Forest School sessions for fellow home edders with the support of Rob in Wood, the owner of our lovely woodland, and my brilliant team of volunteers.
